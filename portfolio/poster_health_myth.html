Building Rich Community with DHR System and using ML for Decision Making ensuring Enriched Health Facility and eradicating Health Myths

The healthcare systems in many developing countries face numerous challenges, including a shortage of doctors, limited financial resources, inadequate medical devices, and insufficient digital infrastructure. People in rural areas especially suffer from a lack of access to healthcare professionals and proper treatment. The absence of a centralized digital system for maintaining medical records further exacerbates these issues. This lack of connectivity and digitalization makes patient data difficult to access and share between different healthcare providers, leading to errors and delays in treatment.

Proposed Solution

A proposed solution involves the creation of a Digital Health Record (DHR) system, which would collect and store patient information such as medical history, diagnoses, medications, treatment plans, immunization details, allergies, lab results, and radiology images. This system would ensure that patient data is available to all relevant healthcare providers, allowing for faster, more accurate diagnoses and reducing the risk of medication errors. The DHR system would be integrated into a web-based platform, enabling healthcare providers across the country to securely share and access patient information. The system would use standard network protocols to ensure interoperability and security, and solar power would be used to ensure consistent energy supply in areas with unreliable electrical grids.

The system would not only improve healthcare access but also enhance healthcare efficiency by using data science and machine learning (ML). Data science would allow administrators to visualize regional health trends, while ML algorithms would predict the risk of diseases in specific areas. This would help healthcare providers take preventive action before diseases spread, improving overall health outcomes. Additionally, the proposed solution includes a decentralized healthcare workforce, where health workers are recruited from within the local community. This approach would address the shortage of healthcare professionals and reduce pressure on urban health centers.

Key Aspects of the system:

  • DHR System: Manages patient records and ensures data is shared securely across health organizations.
  • Predictive Analytics: Uses data science and machine learning to predict health trends and high-risk diseases.
  • Decentralized Workforce: Recruits health workers from local communities to reduce workforce shortages.
  • Solar Power: Ensures energy supply for the system in areas with unreliable electricity.
